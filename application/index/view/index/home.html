Childhood Obesity. A Major Concern.

Obesity is the excessive or abnormal accumilation of fat that may effect health. Childhood obesity is a medical condition which effect children and teenagers. If a child stores too much fat then they are classified as overweight or obese. If left unchecked, obese children grow up to become obese adults and this maybe the reason to many chronic diseases like type A diabetes, cholesterol and heart condition.

Childhood obesity in most cases is a result of children eating too much and not having enough excercise. In some cases obesity can be a result of deeper health issues which have no association to a child's lifestyle. Children who are at the risk of becoming overweight include:
- Children who consume a high quantity of sugary drinks.
- Children who dont excercise enough.
- Who spend most of their time watching TV or playing video games (which does not burn calories)
- Have a lack of good nutrition information.
- Who cannot afford or have access to healthy food.
- Who are suffering from conditions such as Prader-Willi syndrome or Cushing's syndrome.


What Fit Kidz provide.

Fit-kidz is a website which aims at helping combat the issues surrounding child obesity in kids aged between 9-12 years of age so that they grow up to be healthy young adults. This website educates children about the importance of having a healthy diet and leading an active life style.

Key Features

Fit Kidz aims at helping young children between the ages of 9 and 12 years by providing them with the following services

Food Ranking Game

Kids are educated about healthy food choices through food ranking game.

Meal Recommendation

Parents receive their kids meal preferences via email.

Points System

Kids can compete and keep a track of their progress using reward systems and user portal.

Obesity Info

Parents can keep themselves informed about the ill effects of childhood obesity.
